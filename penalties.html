Stay ahead of Employment Equity Penalties

Use this table to understand the maximum fines per section of the Employment Equity Act so you can plan remediating action before penalties apply.

Contravention history Sections 16, 17, 19, 22, 24, 25, 26 and 43 Sections 20, 21, 23 and 44(b)
No previous R 1 500 000 Greater of R 1 500 000 or 2% of T/O
One previous R 1 800 000 Greater of R 1 800 000 or 4% of T/O
Previous within 12 months or two previous R 2 100 000 Greater of R 2 100 000 or 6% of T/O
Three previous for same contravention R 2 400 000 Greater of R 2 400 000 or 8% of T/O
Four previous for same contravention R 2 700 000 Greater of R 2 700 000 or 10% of T/O

Note: “T/O” refers to the employer’s annual turnover. This table is a practical summary; always consult the EEA, regulations, and current government notices for authoritative figures.
